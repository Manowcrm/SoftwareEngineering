Features of Study Buddies

Study Buddies provides essential tools to make studying easier, collaborative, and effective.

Create Your Profile

Set up your study profile with academic interests and availability.

Find Your Buddy

Match with students who share your study goals and subjects.

Chat & Collaborate

Communicate with study partners through our in-app messaging system.

Share Resources

Exchange study materials, notes, and tips with your buddies.

Schedule Study Sessions

Plan and organize study sessions with calendar integration.

Feedback & Ratings

Rate and review study partners to improve recommendations.
