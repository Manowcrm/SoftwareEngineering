How Study Buddies Works

Follow these simple steps to find the perfect study partner and improve your learning experience.

1. Sign Up & Create Your Profile

Register with your email and set up your profile by adding study interests and availability.

2. Find a Study Buddy

Search and connect with students who share similar subjects and learning goals.

3. Start Chatting & Collaborating

Use our built-in messaging feature to discuss topics, share resources, and plan study sessions.

4. Schedule Study Sessions

Plan online or in-person study sessions using the scheduling tool.

5. Improve & Give Feedback

Rate study partners and leave feedback to improve future study connections.
